Courses

Bachelor of Education in Elementary Education(BSED)

This degree is commonly pursued by those who intend to teach at elementary, secondary, and higher secondary levels. The program provides a strong foundation in educational theories, methodologies, pedagogical techniques, and discipline-specific content.

Bachelor of Science in Computing Studies(BSCS)

is the study of computers and algorithmic processes, including their principles, their hardware and software designs, their applications, and their impact on society.

Bachelor of Elementary Education(BEED)

This program focuses on equipping students with the foundational knowledge and teaching skills necessary to effectively educate young learners from kindergarten through the middle grades. BEED is particularly aimed at those who have a passion for shaping the educational experiences of children in their formative years.

Bachelor of Science in Hotel Management(BSHM)

a four-year degree program which focuses on services in hotels, travel and tourism, food and beverage, events and other related segments.
